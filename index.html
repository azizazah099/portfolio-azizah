About Me

Hello! I have a deep passion for various forms of creativity, including drawing, design, singing, and music. These interests fuel my daily inspiration and joy. Writing and reading books are also essential parts of my life, allowing me to explore new ideas and express myself.
Currently, I’m studying Japanese Language and Literature, where I’m immersing myself in the intricacies of the language and its rich literary traditions. This academic journey complements my creative pursuits and helps me connect my artistic passions with my studies. Excited to see where this blend of creativity and scholarship will take me!

I have a deep passion for drawing, especially using IbisPaint. My work primarily focuses on creating simple cartoon illustrations that are brought to life with bright, vibrant colors. I enjoy experimenting with different color palettes and infusing my own unique art style into each piece. Through this medium, I express creativity and develop my artistic voice, continuously evolving my style while staying true to the playful and lively essence of my illustrations.
